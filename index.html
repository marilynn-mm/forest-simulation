Intertwined: A Forest Simulation

Use graph and number theory to simulate the complex dependencies of forest ecosystems.

Modes

  • (1) Prime: Auto-connects to other primes
  • (2) Coprime: Connects to nodes where GCD = 1
  • (3) Modulo 5: Connects to nodes with same mod 5 value
  • (4) Perfect Squares: Connects only to square numbers

What Numbers Mean

Each tree has a number (0 to 99) and connects based on the selected math rule.

The Black Node Rule

If a tree has 0 connections, it turns black. After 2 turns of isolation, it dies and is removed from the forest.
